Cat Walking Animation

Created: September 11th, 2018


A simple spritesheet-like animation of a cat walking. The animation cycles through 12 different custom shapes of a cat in different stages of its walk cycle. The x position of these images increases everytime the draw function is called to give the illusion of walking. The x then resets once the cat is off-screen.



Canvas Source Code - Written in Processing.js (.pde file)
